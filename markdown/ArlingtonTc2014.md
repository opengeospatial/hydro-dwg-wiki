# Agenda

1.  SOS 2.0 Profile for Hydrology - GEOWOW project, with motion for this document to be released as OGC Discussion Paper (52N/Kisters, Michael Utech, remotely) - 20 mins
2.  \<span style="background-color: transparent;"\>USGS’s progress on WaterML2 /SOS services (David Blodgett, USGS) – 15 mins\</span\>
3.  OWS-10 Hydro Model Semantic Interoperability - First Steps. CHISP-2 ideas (Lew Leinenweber, OGC; Chen Yu Hao, GIS.FCU Taiwan) – 15 mins
4.  Discussion of “<a href="WaterML" class="wikilink">WaterML</a>-WQ - an O&M and WaterML 2.0 profile for water quality data”. A vote for this document to be released as OGC Best Practice is requested by Simon Cox. See <https://portal.opengeospatial.org/files/?artifact_id=57014&version=1> , and an earlier briefing at <https://portal.opengeospatial.org/files/?artifact_id=55445>. - 10 mins
5.  GEOSS AIP-6 Water Services project (David Arctur, UT-Austin) - 20 mins
6.  Restarting the Forecasting IE (David Maidment, UT-Austin) – 15 mins
7.  RiverML update (David Arctur, Stephen Jackson) – 20 min
8.  New Zealand Environmental Observation Data Profile (Robert Gibb, LandCare NZ for Jochen Schmidt, NIWA) – 20 min
9.  WaterML 2.0: Part 2 - Ratings, gaugings and sections – Status Update and request to restart WaterML 2.0 SWG to promote adoption of this as OGC standard (BoM/CSIRO - Paul Sheahan/Peter Taylor, remotely) - 10 mins
10. Discussion of renaming WaterML 2.0 Part 1 to TimeSeriesML, motivated by WMO adoption process; requests scope in new WaterML 2.0 SWG charter (Jeremy Tandy, UK Met Office; Frederic Guillaud, Meteo-France) - 10 min
11. Discussion of HydroDWG workshop in August in NYC - deferred to email
