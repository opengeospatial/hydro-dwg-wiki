## **OGC Ocean/Meteo/Hydro Water Cycle Summit,**

## **Wednesday 21-September-2011, Boulder Colorado USA**

Press Release: <http://www.opengeospatial.org/event/1109omh>

#### **Agenda** ([opening slides](https://portal.opengeospatial.org/files/?artifact_id=45963))

1\) [Welcome, Introduction to OGC, and WMO collaboration](https://portal.opengeospatial.org/files/?artifact_id=46180) (D. Arctur) 9-9:15am

2\) [Keynote: whole water cycle, general requirements to information exchange](https://portal.opengeospatial.org/files/?artifact_id=45922) (D. Maidment) 9:15-9:50

3\) [Hydro DWG Intro: purpose, activities and use cases](https://portal.opengeospatial.org/files/?artifact_id=45964) (Ulrich Looser, Peter Fitch, Ilya Zaslavsky) 9:50-10:15

4\) [MetOcean DWG Intro: purpose, activities and use cases](https://portal.opengeospatial.org/files/?artifact_id=45900) (Marie-Françoise Voidrot, Frédéric Guillaud) 10:15-10:40

tea break (10:40-10:50)

5\) Outline for the rest of the day (scene setting talks and breakouts): 10:50-11:00

6\) Scene setting talks: introducing agency and academic perspectives on needs and challenges of information exchange in the hydro-meteo-oceans domains. 11:00 - 12:00

- Results of the Tuesday US Water ad hoc, and the US EPA perspective on needs and challenges (Tod Dabolt)
- [USGS perspective](https://portal.opengeospatial.org/files/?artifact_id=45961) (Nate Booth)
- [Bureau of Meteorology, Australia](https://portal.opengeospatial.org/files/?artifact_id=45960) (Andrew Woolf)
- [UK Flood Forecasting Centre](https://portal.opengeospatial.org/files/?artifact_id=45862) (Jeremy Tandy)

Lunch 12-1pm

7\) Scene setting talks, continued 1:00 - 2:20pm

- [Canada GeoConnections III: Supporting Knowledge Integration](https://portal.opengeospatial.org/files/?artifact_id=45962) (Eric Loubier)
- [National Science Foundation - The Earth Cube initiative](https://portal.opengeospatial.org/files/?artifact_id=45965) (Cliff Jacobs - via WebEx)
- [Catalog/Discovery Challenges Posed by Servers of Real-time Data](https://portal.opengeospatial.org/files/?artifact_id=45881) (Ben Domenico, Unidata/UCAR; Enrico Boldrini, Stefano Nativi, Lorenzo Bigagli, Univ. of Florence/CNR of Italy)
- [New Zealand - NIWA](https://portal.opengeospatial.org/files/?artifact_id=45889) (Brent Wood)

8\) Breakout groups, to discuss needs, challenges and standardization priorities (2:20-4pm)

- Breakout 1: Working together: an experiment to achieve interoperability across the water cycle - Developing ideas for a cross-domain IE.
- Breakout 2: Earth Cube - coordinating a response to the call for use cases, research scenarios, challenges and architecture patterns across earth science domains
- [Breakout 3](https://portal.opengeospatial.org/files/?artifact_id=46116): How can I understand you? Addressing semantic issues between the meteorology, oceans and hydrology communities
- [Breakout 4](https://portal.opengeospatial.org/files/?artifact_id=46097): Data quality, provenance, metadata, uncertainty and trust issues across domains.

9\) A plenary session, with breakout groups reporting, and general discussion: 4:00-5:00pm

Containing folder for all presentations linked above is here: [Meeting Reports Folder for Boulder TC](https://portal.opengeospatial.org/index.php?m=projects&a=view&project_id=82&tab=2&artifact_id=45861)
