## Agenda

1\. Introduction (co-chairs) \[5min\]

2\. Surface Water IE \[9:05-9:50\]

\* Peter Fitch (CSIRO): SWIE status (presented by Carsten Heidmann) \[10min\]

\* Carsten Heidmann (disy): Status of Use Case 1 \[10min\]

\* John Halquist (NOAA/NWS): Use Case 2 Update – Forecasting. Path to Forecasting IE (presented by Stefan Fuest) \[15min\]

\* Stefan Fuest, Michael Natschke (Kisters): Use Case 3 Update \[10min\]

3\. Service interfaces for the IE \[9:50-10:25\]

\* Arne Bröring (52North), Christoph Stasch (Uni Münster): SOS 2.0 update; extending SOS to large data and number of offerings \[20min\]

\* Uwe Voges (con terra GmbH): CS3.0 status report \[15min\]

Break \[10:25-10:30\]

4\. Pete Taylor (CSIRO) and co-chairs: WaterML 2.0 update and report on SWG status \[15min\]

5\. Roger Moore, Quillon Harpham (OpenMI): Towards standards-based hydrologic modeling: OpenMI path to OGC standardization \[15min\]

6\. Antonio Parodi, Roberto Rudari (CIMA Foundation): Hydro-Meteorology Research and ICT: DEWETRA, OWIS and DRIHMS experience \[20min\] See the abstract of the presentation below.

7\. Luis Bermudez (OGC): OGC Interoperability Program Concept Development Study \[20min\]

This study is part of the process of developing a Pilot Initiative for Water Information Services. An outcome of the CD study will be an Engineering Report that will be a basis for the architecture in the Pilot. Working currently with D. Maidment and OGC staff to develop a Call for Sponsors for the Pilot initiative; anticipating that the Draft ER and the CFS will be issued before Bonn.

8\. Gilles Ollier (European Commission, Directorate General Research): European GEO/GEOSS related activities \[10min\]

Discussion and motions \[10min\]
