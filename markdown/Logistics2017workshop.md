## \<a name="Logistics"\>\</a\> Logistics, accommodation, admin

#### Logistics

**Overseas visitors will need to complete this [form](http://www.osec.doc.gov/osy/NOAA/Forms/foreign%20visitor%20form.pdf) by 1 June 2017 in order to be able to attend the workshop at NWC, NOAA. Please submit your completed form to Dave Blodgett (<dblodgett@usgs.gov>) by this date.**

The [National Water Center](http://water.noaa.gov/about/nwc), Office of Water Prediction, NOAA is in Tuscaloosa Alabama on the University of Alabama campus.

You should plan to fly into [Birmingham and rent a car or share a ride to Tuscaloosa](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/birmingham+airport/Tuscaloosa/@33.3603204,-87.7199467,9z/am=t/data=!3m1!4b1!4m13!4m12!1m5!1m1!1s0x88891aa4f6775025:0x9326a00858ed2b68!2m2!1d-86.754126!2d33.5624269!1m5!1m1!1s0x8888af2e91c24067:0xacabfbfd16892b58!2m2!1d-87.5691735!2d33.2098407). The drive is very easy, about 1 hour, but there are few options other than driving.

Flying into [Atlanta and driving to Tuscaloosa](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/atlanta+airport/Tuscaloosa/@33.0842016,-88.2192776,7z/am=t/data=!3m1!4b1!4m13!4m12!1m5!1m1!1s0x88f4fd2fe1035901:0x4117a3ef1892b048!2m2!1d-84.4277001!2d33.6407282!1m5!1m1!1s0x8888af2e91c24067:0xacabfbfd16892b58!2m2!1d-87.5691735!2d33.2098407) is also an option (3 hr drive) that could avoid domestic flight complications since Atlanta is a major international hub.

Please use \<a href="Attendees2017workshop" target="\_blank"\>this page\</a\> to record your travel plans and coordinate rides.
