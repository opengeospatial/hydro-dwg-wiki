\<h3 id='<a href="A_42Meeting_Agenda_and_Outcomes_42" class="wikilink">A_42Meeting_Agenda_and_Outcomes_42</a>'\> **Meeting Agenda and Outcomes** \</h3\>

- Silvano Pecora (WMO) - WMO long-term ambition: "Water quality is known": [Link to the Presentation](%ATTACHURL%/HDWG_20210317.pptx)
- Philipp Saile (ICWRGC, GEMS/Water) - In-situ WQ sampling data sharing requirements and use cases
- Steve Groom (Plymouth Marine Lab) - Standards/services and tools used by the remote sensing WQ community: the [CERTO project](https://engage.certo-project.org/data/)
- James Kreft (USGS) and Dwane Young (EPA) - Water Quality Data Sharing - U.S EPA's Water Quality Exchange : [Link to the Presentation](%ATTACHURL%/WQX_Background.pptx)
- \<span style='background-color: transparent;'\>Sylvain Grellet (BRGM / INSIDE - environmental information systems research center) - 2 tests to serve French surface water quality DB data : one \</span\>\<span style='background-color: transparent;'\>using <a href="SemanticWeb" class="wikilink">SemanticWeb</a> technologies (see [Ninth HydroDWG Workshop presentation](%PUBURL%/HydrologyDWG/WorkshopGeneva2018/20180919_water_vocabs_experimentation_on_freshwater_quality_Grellet.pptx)) \</span\>\<span style='background-color: transparent;'\>and one \</span\>[OGC SensorThings API](https://www.ogc.org/standards/sensorthings) : [Link to the Presentation](%ATTACHURL%/20210317_WQ_INSIDE_BRGM_Grellet.pptx)
- Bruce Simons (Federation University) - [WQ best practice document](http://docs.opengeospatial.org/bp/14-003/14-003.html) overview and relationship to existing OGC standards : [Link to the Presentation](%ATTACHURL%/WaterQuality_Simons.pptx)
- General discussion on the way forward/ next steps/ group of experts to progress...
