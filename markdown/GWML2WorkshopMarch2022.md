\<h2 id='<a href="Hydrology_Domain_Working_Group" class="wikilink">Hydrology_Domain_Working_Group</a>'\>Hydrology Domain Working Group\</h2\> \<h2 id='<a href="Meeting_details" class="wikilink">Meeting_details</a>:'\>Workshop topic: Implementing GWML2\</h2\>

Venue: Online TBA Time: March 2022, 19:00 – 21:30 (UTC)

### Objectives

This workshop covered:

- Introduction to GWML2 standard and its scope
- Demonstrations of the GWML2 implementation from France, Canada, United States, Australia and New Zealand
- Panel discussion on the benefits of GWML2 implementation
- Moderated discussion on further GWML2 implementation
