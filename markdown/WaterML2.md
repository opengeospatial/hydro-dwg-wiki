WaterML 2.0 Part 1 - Time Series has been adopted as an international standard in the Open Geospatial Consortium as of June 2012, see \<a href="<http://www.opengeospatial.org/standards/waterml>" target="\_blank"\><http://www.opengeospatial.org/standards/waterml>\</a\>

In bringing this subsequently to the World Meteorological Organization for co-adoption, the WMO suggested the time series description and usage was more general than just for water, and requested that WaterML 2.0 Part 1 be the basis for a TimeseriesML standard, which could be used by multiple domains such as hydro, atmospheric, ocean and climate. This is now underway in the Timeseries SWG, see \<a href="<https://portal.opengeospatial.org/?m=projects&a=view&project_id=470>" target="\_blank"\>OGC portal\</a\> for more information (member login required; if you're an OGC member but don't have access, you may need to become an Observer on this SWG, which you can do \<a href="<https://portal.opengeospatial.org/?m=public&orderby=default&tab=6>" target="\_blank"\>here\</a\>).

-- Main.<a href="DavidArctur" class="wikilink">DavidArctur</a> - 26 Aug 2015
