### **Wednesday, 13-17:00 (Room: 1)**

1.  Introduction (HDWG co-chairs) (5 min)
2.  WMO Update, and discussion (Uli Looser, David Maidment, 30 mins)
3.  World Water Online (David Maidment, David Arctur, 20 mins)

5.  Status of CHISP-1 Pilot (Nate Booth, Lew Leineweber, 20 mins)
6.  AIP-5 Water SBA results (David Maidment, Matt Austin, 20 mins)

11. IE discussion: Forecasting, GWIE2 (IE leaders, 40 min)
