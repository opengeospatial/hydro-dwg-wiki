# WaterML 2.0 part 2 - Ratings, Gaugings and Sections (RGS)

As of August 2015, the OGC TC & PC votes for adoption have been completed, and WaterML 2.0 Part 2 - RGS is now an OGC international standard.

This page is for information relating to the development of WaterML 2.0 part 2 to address ratings, gaugings and potentially cross sections.
