# Agenda

### **Monday, 15:45 - 18:40 (Room: E)**

1.  Introduction, [agenda](%ATTACHURL%/2013_Frascati_HDWG_Agenda.ppt), review of the [Quebec workshop outcomes](%ATTACHURL%/Outcomes_Quebec_Workshop.ppt) (HDWG co-chairs) (10 min)
2.  Update on [GEOSS AIP-6 Water Services project](%ATTACHURL%/GEOSS_AIP-6_Water_Services_Update.pptx) (David Arctur, 15-30 mins)
3.  [CHISP-1 Brief Review](%ATTACHURL%/CHISP-1_Summary_and_Outcomes.pptx)- Architecture, Achievements and Future Work (summary of ER) (Lew Leinenweber, 15 min)
    - Possible motion to recommend to the TC to release the ER as a public Engineering Report
4.  [WaterML2.0: Part 1](%ATTACHURL%/WaterML2.0_Part_1_Survey.ppt): Usage by hydrological organisations and system vendors (Tony Boston, 15 mins)
5.  [Open source solutions](%ATTACHURL%/Open_source_federated_systems.ppt) for federated hydrologic information systems (Silvano Pecora, 15 min)
6.  Representing physical and semantic environment for flow simulation.in [CityGML](%ATTACHURL%/CityGML_Hydrology.ppt) - towards a joint use case (Steve Smyth, 20 mins)
7.  Ratings, gaugings and cross sections [(WaterML2.0 part 2) Interoperability Experiment](%ATTACHURL%/WaterML2_Part2_Interoperability_Experiment.ppt) - status update (David Lemon, 15 mins)
8.  <a href="WaterML" class="wikilink">WaterML</a> -WQ - a [WaterML2 profile for water quality](%ATTACHURL%/Water_Quality_data_transfer.pptx), with a water quality vocabulary (Simon Cox, 20 Mins)
9.  Progress on [HY_Features](%ATTACHURL%/2013_Frascati_HY_Features_Progress_.ppt) (Irina Dornblut, 15 min)
10. Progress on [WMO (GRDC) Hydrologic Metadata](%ATTACHURL%/GRDC_Hydrologic_Metadata_Progress.ppt) (Irina Dornblut, 15 min)
