<h2 id='Hydrology_Domain_Working_Group'>Hydrology Domain Working Group</h2> <h2 id='Meeting_details:'>Workshop topic: Implementing GWML2</h2>

Venue: Online TBA Time: March 2022, 19:00 &ndash; 21:30 (UTC)

---+++ Objectives

This workshop covered:
   * Introduction to GWML2 standard and its scope
   * Demonstrations of the GWML2 implementation from France, Canada, United States, Australia and New Zealand
   * Panel discussion on the benefits of GWML2 implementation
   * Moderated discussion on further GWML2 implementation
