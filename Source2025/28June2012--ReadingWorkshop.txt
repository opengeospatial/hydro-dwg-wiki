---+ *GWIE2 Breakout Session,* *Hydrology DWG Workshop 2012*

This workshop continues the directions outlined in the successful [[12April2011--DelftWorkshop][Delft Workshop]], in which it was agreed to move to a harmonized international GML schema for groundwater data. Since that [[12April2011--DelftWorkshop][Delft Workshop]], significant progress has been made by INSPIRE and OGC/WMO on schemas that include groundwater elements, and the GWML1 schema has been extensively tested in N.America. In this workshop we will review these developments and chart a concrete plan toward an OGC/WMO standard.
