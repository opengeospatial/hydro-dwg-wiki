---++ Agenda



1. Introduction, results of Ispra workshop (co-chairs), IEs and WaterML (approximately 8-9:50am)

   * 8-8:20: David Lemon, HydroDWG planning and next steps 

   * 8:20-9:40: WaterML 2 and Harmonization report updates (Pete Taylor and the WaterML design group)

   * 9:40-9:50 Groundwater IE update; Surface Water IE announcement and kick-off invitation (Peter Fitch)

2. Presentations (10-11:45am)

   * 10-10:25: Mark Nardi (USGS) - Water use data management at USGS: information model, implementations, prospects of water use science.

   * 10:24-10:45: Roland Viger and Nate Booth (USGS) - Using existing and emerging OGC standards to support hydrologic models.

   * 10:45-11:05: Ben Domenico (UNIDATA) - Managing point time series in netCDF/DAP

   * 11:05-11:25: John Halquist (NOAA), Peter Gijsbers (Deltares) -  Operational hydrologic forecasts and infrastructure operation: use cases and requirements

   * 11:25-11:45: Michael Piasecki (Drexel University), Ilya Zaslavsky (SDSC) -  CUAHSI Hydrologic parameter ontology

3. Discussion and motions (11:45-12)
