https://xp-dev.com/svn/gwml2/Documents/schematron/

sch file turned into XSLT that generate SVRL (rule test results)

   * [[http://gin.gw-info.net/service/schematron/gen_xslt_gwml/xml-rules.xslt][xml-rules.xslt]] Must be passed by all instances
   * [[http://gin.gw-info.net/service/schematron/gen_xslt_gwml/gwml2-well.xslt][gwml2-well.xslt]] specific for wells
   * [[http://gin.gw-info.net/service/schematron/gen_xslt_gwml/gwml2-well-vertical.xslt][gwml2-well-vertical.xslt]] specific for vertical wells
   * [[http://gin.gw-info.net/service/schematron/gen_xslt_gwml/gwml2-well-gu.xslt][gwml2-well-gu.xslt]] Geologic unit logs
   * [[http://gin.gw-info.net/service/schematron/gen_xslt_gwml/gwml2-well-em.xslt][gwml2-well-em.xslt]] Earth material logs
   * [[http://gin.gw-info.net/service/schematron/gen_xslt_gwml/gwml2-construction.xslt][gwml2-construction.xslt]] Well construction
