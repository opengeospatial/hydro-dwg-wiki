---++ <a href="http://www.koblenz-tourism.com/" target="_blank">Things to do</a>

Koblenz offers <a href="https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attractions-g187391-Activities-Koblenz_Rhineland_Palatinate.html" target="_blank">numerous attractions</a>. It is a favourite tourist destination located at the confluence of the Rhine and Mosel rivers within the UNESCO World Heritage Site  <a href="http://whc.unesco.org/en/list/1066" target="_blank">"Upper Middle Rhine Valley"</a>. Apart from visiting some interesting spots in town, take a trip with the <a href="http://www.seilbahn-koblenz.de/home-page.html" target="_blank">cable car </a>across the Rhine to the Fortress Ehrenbreitstein for a stunning view of the <a href="http://www.bafg.de/EN/06_Info_Service/03_Webcam_en/webcam_en_node.html" target="_blank">confluence of the Rhine and Moselle rivers </a>called "Deutsches Eck". Enjoy a boat trip and/or the world famous wines from <a href="http://www.germany.travel/en/towns-cities-culture/gemuetlichkeit/wine-country/winegrowing-regions/middle-rhine.html" target="_blank">Rhine</a> and <a href="http://www.germany.travel/en/towns-cities-culture/gemuetlichkeit/wine-country/winegrowing-regions/moselle.html" target="_blank">Moselle</a> vineyards.
