---++ Announcement

*Dates:*      15 - 18 March, 2010 (Formal component on 16th and 17th)

*Venue:*    JRC, Ispra, Italy

*Purpose:*

This meeting will be the first workshop of the Hydrology Domain Working Group. It is open to any member of the working group.

The kind folk at JRC in Ispra (Italy) have offered to host us in March in the week following the Frascati meeting of the OGC Technical Committee meeting. That is, the week starting March 15. The main meeting will be held on the 16th and 17th with side meetings on the 15th and 18th.

The workshop represents an excellent opportunity for the Hydro DWG to get some real work done. In particular, it presents a great opportunity to have a gathering for those interested in the development of a water data exchange format (along the lines proposed in the CSIRO/CUAHSI Harmonisation report) as well as those involved in the current Groundwater and proposed Surface Water IEs.
