---+++ Description

Test !WaterML2.0 with surface water data by disseminating surface water data from Rhin - Rhein - between Germany & France to all participants using OGC SOS, WFS, WMS web services and !WaterML2.0 encoding. This will additionally demonstrate cross border surface water data interoperability.

---+++ Participants and contact persons
   * DLZ-IT: German Federal Waterways Engineering and Research Institute (Dietmar Mothes)
   * IOW-Sandre: International Office for Water - Sandre (Sylvain Grellet)
   * KISTERS AG (Michael Natschke, Stefan Fuest)
   * 52north (Arne Bröring, Simon Jirka)
   * disy Informationssysteme (Carsten Heidmann)

---+++ Contributing countries

There are no contacts yet to Switzerland. The Netherlands are kept informed (Contact to Martijn van Dijk from Rijkswaterstaat by Carsten Heidmann and Bernd Mörstedt (DLZ-IT)) about the progress of the IE.
