---++ Announcement

*Dates:* 11 - 14 April, 2011

*Venue:* UNESCO-IHE, Delft, The Netherlands

*Purpose:*

This meeting will be the second workshop of the Hydrology Domain Working Group. It is open to any member of the working group.

The kind people at UNESCO-IHE Institute for Water Education in Delft (The Netherlands) have offered to host us in April in the week following EGU and the week before the [[http://hikm.ihe.nl/openwater_eg/][OpenWater Symposium]]. That is, the week starting April 11.

The workshop represents an excellent opportunity for the Hydro DWG to progress the excellent work done so far. In particular, it presents an opportunity to have a gathering for those interested in: !WaterML2.0, Surface Water IE, Forecasting IE, GWML2.0, a Surface Water feature model, standard water vocubularies.
